Privacy & Safety

Safety-first rules we follow on every post.

Public commitment

This site is built for long-term growth while protecting our family’s privacy.

  • No real names or personal identifiers
  • No real-time location sharing
  • No schedules or travel details
  • Content shared intentionally and retrospectively
  • Privacy and safety always come first

Media checklist (before posting)

  • Crop out signs, logos, license plates, and other children’s faces
  • Remove location metadata (EXIF) before upload
  • Post after events — never real time
  • If unsure, don’t post
