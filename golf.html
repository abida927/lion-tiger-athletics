Golf Journey

Golf is our primary focus. We document process, patience, and steady improvement — shared with safety and intention.

What we share (safe)

  • Practice themes (e.g., tempo, routine, short game)
  • Mental approach and consistency habits
  • Post‑event tournament reflections (after completion)
  • Long‑term goals (no dates or locations)

What we don’t share

  • Real names, birthdays, school details
  • Real‑time travel, schedules, or locations
  • Identifying background signage
